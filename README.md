# 📊 Port Operations & Cargo Efficiency Analysis - Panama

An interactive **Power BI** dashboard focused on logistics and container movement across Panama's strategic Atlantic and Pacific coasts.

This project transforms raw maritime shipping data into actionable executive insights to optimize routes, identify costly logistics inefficiencies (empty container repositioning), and track seasonal demand fluctuations.

## 💡 Key Business Insights

*   **Atlantic Coast Dominance:** The Atlantic coast leads operations with **5.1 million containers** moved, significantly outperforming the Pacific coast (**3.2 million**). The report utilizes *Drill Down* capabilities to audit individual port contributions (e.g., MIT, Cristobal, CCT).
*   **Reverse Logistics Bottlenecks:** A staggering **47.31%** of all empty container movements—a major operational cost sink—is concentrated in a single port. Targeting this specific terminal for equipment-sharing strategies is the highest-value cost-saving opportunity.
*   **Highly Seasonal Demand:** **Transshipment** cargo (the main business driver) experiences a sharp acceleration starting in July, peaking in September to prepare for the end-of-year peak retail season. In contrast, **Local** traffic remains completely stable and predictable year-round.
*   **70.95% Operational Efficiency:** Designed a high-impact KPI metric measuring the percentage of loaded (revenue-generating) vs. empty containers, allowing executives to monitor overall fleet utilization at a single glance.

## 🛠️ Technical Skills Applied

1.  **Data Cleansing & ETL (Power Query):** Normalized schema layouts, resolved circular dependencies in date tables, and optimized data types to ensure high file performance.
2.  **Data Modeling & DAX:** Built dedicated calendar dimension tables and wrote custom DAX measures to calculate cargo efficiency ratios and dynamic regional groupings.
3.  **UI/UX Design (Dark Mode):** Applied clean data design principles, removing axis clutter and gridlines to guide the user's eye directly to key performance indicators (KPIs).
